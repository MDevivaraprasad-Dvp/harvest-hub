03 · The solution FarmEasy

One app. Three pillars.
Farmers keep the value.

Direct marketplace

Buyers browse fresh produce with real farm photos, filter by location and category, and negotiate price directly with the farmer. No middleman, no markup.

Live orders + negotiation

Contract farming

Businesses pre-fund a harvest — locking in supply and price before planting. Farmers get income certainty; buyers get guaranteed delivery.

Full lifecycle: open → completed

AI + human network

AI handles quick questions; human expert farmers and agri-engineers handle the complex ones. Affordable for farmers, sustainable for us.

Hybrid by design
Marketplace Contract farming AI + human help Farmer analytics 4 languages

05 · How it flows FarmEasy

Two roles. Two journeys.
One shared marketplace.

Farmer journey

From field to funded

  1. Sign in with name + phone — no email, no password
  2. Post a listing with a photo taken from the field camera
  3. Get order alerts — accept, reject, or counter-offer on price
  4. Track views, orders, conversion — see which listings work
  5. Accept contract farming offers from restaurants and businesses
  6. Ask the AI + human knowledge network anything, any time
Buyer journey

  5. Post a contract farming request — pre-fund your supply

06 · AI + Human FarmEasy

AI is powerful.
But it shouldn't replace the humans who feed us.

Pure-AI apps sound futuristic but scale poorly for smallholder farmers. Every LLM call costs money — and if the AI is free and unlimited, either the company bleeds cash or the farmer pays a premium they can't afford. Neither works. So we split the load.

◉ What AI handles

Fast, free, first-response

  • Quick FAQ — crop basics, pest ID, seasonal advice
  • 5 free questions per day per farmer (quota keeps costs bounded)
  • Language translation on the fly across 4 languages
  • Market price summaries from listing data
◉ What humans handle

Deep, local, accountable

  • Complex crop diagnosis with photo + local soil context
  • Field visits by agri-engineers when it matters
  • Dialect + hyperlocal knowledge AI simply doesn't have
  • Premium tier (₹299/mo) — one-tap call to verified experts
Farmer cost
Free tier for FAQ. Only pay for deep expert help — when it's worth it.
Company cost
Bounded LLM spend per user. AI stays a tool, not a runaway line item.
Human jobs
Agri-engineers keep their livelihoods. The app amplifies them, doesn't replace.
Better answers
Local soil, dialect, climate context is human. AI does the routine.
07 · Why this works FarmEasy

Farmer-first design.
Not a Western app translated.

01

Built for the phone in the field

Camera-only capture opens the rear camera on mobile. Phone-number identity — no email, no password, no OTP wall to fail on 2G.

02

Contract farming is native

Restaurants pre-fund a harvest with a down payment. Farmers see committed income before they plant. Nobody else in this space does this.

03

AI + human hybrid, on purpose

We limit AI to keep costs affordable and to preserve agri-engineer jobs. Sustainable economics, not hype.

04

Multilingual from day one

English, Hindi, Telugu, Tamil — every string, every error, every toast. Not an afterthought translation layer.

05

Works on every screen — one codebase

Fluid from 375px phones to 1440px desktops. Sidebar collapses to a hamburger drawer on mobile, marketplace grid flows 1→2→3→4 columns, every modal, form and toast is tuned for touch and mouse. No separate mobile app, no compromised desktop view.
